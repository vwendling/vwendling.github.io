Different Gymnastic Events

pages1 pages2 pages3

Gymnastics is a competitive sport that incoporates many events within it. These events include...

  • Floor
  • Beam
  • Bars
  • Vault
You recieve a score for each event. The scores which the gymnasts recieve deterimines their overall ranking for their placemet in the competition. The best score to the worst scores will be listed below.

Scoring

  1. 10.00
    • 9.1-9.9 can also be achieved
    • 9.5 to 9.9 does not round to 10.00
    • 9.1 to 9.4 does not round to 9.00
  2. 9.00
    • 8.1-8.9 can also be achieved
    • 8.5 to 8.9 does not round to 10.00
    • 8.1 to 8.4 does not round to 8.00
  3. 8.00
    • 7.1-7.9 can also be achieved
    • 7.5 to 7.9 does not round to 8.00
    • 7.1 to 7.4 does not round to 7.00
  4. 7.00
    • 6.1-6.9 can also be achieved
    • 6.5 to 6.9 does not round to 7.00
    • 6.1 to 6.4 does not round to 6.00
  5. 6.00
    • 5.1-5.9 can also be achieved
    • 5.5 to 5.9 does not round to 6.00
    • 5.1 to 5.4 does not round to 5.00
  6. 5.00
Nothing under 5 is usually scored at competitions. Doing the basic requirements in a routine will give you a 5 out of 10, however if you do not meet the specific requirements for example a jump or a turn you could recieve a score below 5 or < 5.
